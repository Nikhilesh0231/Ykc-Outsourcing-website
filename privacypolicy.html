Information Security & Confidentiality Assurance

YKC takes security and confidentiality of client information very seriously and that is why we have implemented stringent security safeguards and processes as per GDPR for every information touch point Since our processes are clear and well defined, we are able to immediately detect and handle any information breach and provide 360°security.Our security process comprises of four stages Planning, Protection, Detection, and Response These four stages drill down to all security aspects related to People, Technology, Physical, and Network infrastructures.
